a bit about my programming journey, what attaracted me to founders and coders, and why i am applying for the course:

1. hello! im milena, a multimedia artist and budding programmer. before the pandemic i created interactive installation that encouraged people to play and forget about their worries. then covid happened and like everyone else i was stuck indoors, with my laptop as a means of entertainment - i began to explore how i can move my practice to the digital sphere?

2. that's when i discovered computer programming and became obsessed! i enjoyed it so much i decided to seek a career in the field and started gaining certifications through free code camp. instead of making sculptures, i became motivated by writing and designing useful code for people to use

3. one of my main objectives is creating safe spaces in tech for queer people - i would like to create games and vr environments that are inclusive and progressive - queer spaces made for queer people by queer people

4. founders and coders was highly recommended to me by a friend of an alumni as a great place to advance my programming skills, as well as have a better chance of landing a great job in tech, and starting a meaningful career

5. finally - i'd like to meet new people with similar interests and create really interesting projects!
